Healthy Eating

Healthy eating is important for maintaining a balanced diet and overall well-being.

A balanced meal provides the necessary nutrients for optimal health and energy levels.

Smart Snack Choices

Choosing the right snacks can help you maintain a healthy diet and avoid unnecessary calories.

Opt for snacks that are rich in nutrients.

Snacks can be healthy when we choose foods that give energy instead of only sugar. Fruits, nuts, leafy greens, and yogurt are all great options for smart snacking.

Nutrition Tips for Students

Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is essential for maintaining good health and cognitive function.

Water helps the body stay active, supports digestion, and keeps the brain focused. Students should carry a water bottle and drink water during the day to stay hydrated, especially during exams and physical activities.

Eat colourful Foods

Different colours in fruits and vegetables provide a range of vitamins and minerals.

Aim to include a variety of colours in your diet to ensure you're getting a wide range of nutrients.
